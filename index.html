About Harmony Valley Academy

Our school is committed to fostering a supportive and engaging environment where every student can achieve their full potential. We believe in holistic education that balances academic rigor with character development and community involvement.

We boast a **low student-to-teacher ratio**, ensuring personalized attention, and a rich history of serving the local community for over 50 years.

Admissions Information

We welcome applications from families within our community. The admission process is designed to be simple and transparent.

  • **Step 1:** Review requirements and download the application package.
  • **Step 2:** Schedule a mandatory campus tour and information session.
  • **Step 3:** Submit the completed application before the **January 31st deadline**.
